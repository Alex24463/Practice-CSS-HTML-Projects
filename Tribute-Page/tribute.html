Son Goku

Defender of Earth

Son Goku is Earth's mightiest warrior and battles a wide variety of villains with his friends and family.

Here's a time line of Son Goku's life:

  • Birth (Age 737):Goku is born as Kakarot on Planet Vegeta, the son of Bardock and Gine
  • Destruction of Planet Vegeta (Age 737):Frieza destroys Planet Vegeta, leading to the near-extinction of the Saiyan race. Goku is sent to Earth, where he suffers a head injury and forgets his Saiyan mission
  • Goku's Childhood (Age 749 - 756): Raised by Grandpa Gohan, Goku discovers his Saiyan heritage, trains in martial arts, and participates in the 21st and 22nd World Martial Arts Tournaments
  • Training with Master Roshi (Age 750 - 753):Goku trains under Master Roshi, learning the Kamehameha and participating in various martial arts tournaments
  • Meeting and Training with King Kai (Age 753 - 756): Goku trains in Other World, mastering the Kaio-Ken and Spirit Bomb techniques, and eventually returns to Earth
  • Frieza Saga (Age 762): Goku arrives on Planet Namek, achieves Super Saiyan form, and defeats Frieza in an epic battle
  • Android Saga (Age 767): Goku faces new threats, including androids created by Dr. Gero and the bio-engineered Cell. Gohan, Goku's son, ultimately defeats Cell
  • Babidi Saga (Age 774): Goku, Gohan, and Vegeta confront the wizard Babidi and his creation, Majin Buu, leading to the emergence of Kid Buu
  • Battle of Gods Saga (Age 778): The god of destruction, Beerus, arrives, and Goku attains the power of Super Saiyan God during the battle
  • Resurrection 'F' Saga (Age 779):Frieza returns, and Goku achieves the Super Saiyan Blue form during the battle to defend Earth
  • Universe 6 Saga (Age 779): Goku participates in the Universe 6 vs. Universe 7 tournament, introducing new powerful characters like Hit
  • Future Trunks Saga (Age 780):Goku faces the threat of Goku Black and Future Zamasu, leading to the discovery of the Super Saiyan Rose form
  • Tournament of Power (Age 780): Goku participates in the multiverse Tournament of Power, fighting to save Universe 7 from erasure
  • Broly Saga (Age 780): Goku encounters the powerful Saiyan, Broly, leading to an intense battle and the expansion of the Dragon Ball lore
  • Current Events (Age 780 - Present):Goku continues training and participating in various events, with the series transitioning into Dragon Ball Super and potentially exploring new story arcs beyond the Tournament of Power and the Broly Saga
